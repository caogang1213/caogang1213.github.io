I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Massey University in 2017. My research background and interest include:
  • Machine Learning: deep learning, Bayesian learning, artificial neural network, reinforcement learning
  • Computer Vision: object recognition, classficiation, detection and tracking, image processing and analysis
  • Intelligent Control: model predictive control, adaptive control, optimal and robust control, system modelling, parameter identification, sensor fusion
  • Numerical Optimization: non-/convex optimization, stochastic optimization, evolutionary algorithm, dynamic programming
  • Pattern Recognition: data-driven modelling and prediction, un-/supervised learning, feature engineering, data engineering
